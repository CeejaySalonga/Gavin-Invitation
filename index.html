Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

+

Please arrive between 1:30-2:00 PM. The baptism ceremony will begin promptly at 2:00 PM.

Is there parking available?

+

Yes! Free parking is available in the cathedral parking lot and on nearby streets.

What should I wear?

+

Sunday best attire is requested. Modest and respectful clothing appropriate for a sacred ceremony is recommended.

Can I bring my children?

+

Absolutely! Children are welcome to attend this special celebration. We encourage families to come together.

+

Yes, there will be a celebration reception with refreshments in the parish hall following the ceremony.

Is this a religious ceremony?

+

Yes, this is a Christian baptism ceremony. All are welcome to attend and celebrate this sacred milestone.
